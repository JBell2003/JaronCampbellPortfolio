About

I’m a Media and Communications graduate currently completing a Master’s in Journalism and Communication, building a body of work across video, audio, and the web. My background combines reporting, scripting, shooting, and editing, so I’m comfortable taking a story from first idea through to final cut.

In both coursework and professional experience, I’ve helped plan and promote events, created digital campaigns, and produced content for local organizations and communities. I’m especially interested in collaborative newsrooms and creative teams where storytelling, design, and audience strategy overlap.
